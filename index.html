About Me

I have a background in Hair, Makeup, and Fashion merchandising.
I'm a licensed cosmetologist. With over two decades of experience in professional makeup artistry, I have made a name for myself working in film, print, beauty and the fashion industry.

Currently working as a freelance artist, it has given me many opportunities to work with some of the industry's top experts in a variety of fields.. film, theater, cinema, video, and photography.

I love being able to create a long-lasting look for a fabulous face. A look that not only pleases the client, but the talent as well. They have to feel good to look good on camera.

Using the best professional products, MAC, NARS, Makeup Forever, Laura Mercier, Smashbox and many more.
To sum it up…. I LOVE MY JOB!!
